Relocate Tracks

Update track file paths in your Rekordbox library — for example, when upgrading from MP3 to FLAC, or after moving your music folder. Cues, beatgrids, and playlists are always preserved.

⚠ Close Rekordbox before running. A timestamped backup of master.db is created automatically before any changes are written.
