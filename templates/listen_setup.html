Setup Guide
Step 1 — Install Tailscale on this PC
Download from tailscale.com and sign in with a free account.
Tailscale creates a private encrypted tunnel between your devices — no port forwarding needed.
Step 2 — Install Tailscale on your iPhone
Search "Tailscale" in the App Store. It's free.
Sign in with the same account you used on your PC.
Step 3 — Set a listener PIN
Go to Setup & Paths and enter a 4-digit PIN under Remote Listener.
This PIN protects access to your music from your phone.
Step 4 — When you're ready to head out
1. Close Rekordbox if it's open.
2. Leave this app running (minimise the window — don't close it).
3. On your iPhone, open the listener URL in Safari and enter your PIN.
Why Tailscale? Your audio files stay on your home PC — nothing is uploaded to any cloud service. Tailscale routes your iPhone's requests directly to this server over an encrypted private connection. Only devices signed into your Tailscale account can connect.
